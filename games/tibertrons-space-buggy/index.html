Deep beyond charted space, you pilot the last scouting buggy from world to world to keep your ship alive. Each orbit reveals new planets rich with mineral veins and genetic traces, but every landing is a risk. Controls: Auto-scroll right. Left/Right adjust speed. Jump timing clears craters. Aim follows mouse/touch point, click to fire, plus bottom-left Fire button on mobile.
