🌌 How to Use 3D Dimension

  • Depth — How much the stroke extrudes in 3D
  • X Angle / Y Angle — Tilt direction of the 3D effect
  • Z Offset — Moves the extruded layer closer/farther
  • Perspective — Controls depth distortion
