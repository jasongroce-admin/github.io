Four Circles

Connect-four style gameplay against an AI Bot.

You Red circles
AI Bot Gold circles
Tap or drag a chip, then tap a column.

Match Setup

Your move. Tap/drag a red chip, then choose a column.

How It Plays

  • You play first as red.
  • Medium blocks immediate wins and prefers strong columns.
  • Hard uses deeper lookahead (minimax) for stronger play.
