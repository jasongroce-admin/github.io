Road Hazard Tips

  • Soft spots: discolored asphalt with bubbling brown water.
  • Debris: limbs, cones, bins, and shopping carts can spin your cruiser.
  • People: pedestrians may dart from sidewalks.
  • Practice mode: no call timer, great for learning the streets.

Dispatch Ready

Choose a mode. Mission mode sends you to timed 4-digit house calls on town streets. Free Roam removes the timer so you can practice.

  • Drive with arrow keys / WASD or touch controls.
  • Hold SIREN for a short boost.
  • Avoid traffic, potholes, limbs, trash bins, carts, oil, and open manholes.
  • Collect clocks and shields for time and hull recovery.
