KPD Hazard Tips

  • Soft spots: discolored asphalt with bubbling brown water.
  • Debris: limbs, cones, bins, and carts can spin your cruiser.
  • Forward + reverse: use Up/Down to control pace through turns.
  • Boost: short taps are snappy and drop off quickly.

Dispatch Ready

Choose a mode. Mission mode sends timed house calls. Free Roam removes the timer to practice curved road handling.

  • Drive with arrow keys / WASD or touch controls.
  • Move left, right, forward, and back to manage curves.
  • Hold SIREN for quick bursts of speed.
  • Avoid hazards and collect clocks/shields when it is safe.
